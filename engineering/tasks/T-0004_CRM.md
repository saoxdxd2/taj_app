Title: CRM

Business Objective

Establish the CRM domain to strictly manage and protect customer and contact information. Customers form the basis for sales, quotes, and communications within TAJ FROID.

Engineering Objective

Implement the `Customer` and `Address` models mapped via SQLAlchemy relationships, enforcing the Soft Delete Policy (archiving) rather than hard deletion, matching the criteria in `10_BUSINESS_ARCHITECTURE.md` and `11_DATABASE_STANDARD.md`.

- Soft Delete Policy is enforced for the Customer lifecycle.
